## AI-generated imagery (local, commercial-safe) — `public/photos/gen-*.png`

Generated locally via the `assetfactory` engine with **Stable Diffusion XL base 1.0**
(`sd_xl_base_1.0`), whose license permits commercial distribution of outputs. No people
are depicted — purely thematic industrial scenes.

- `gen-rooftop-ahu.png` — "industrial building rooftop with rows of large galvanized steel
  air handling units and HVAC extraction fans, blue hour twilight sky, photorealistic, no
  people". Used as the hero background.
- `gen-spiral-ductwork.png` — "long run of clean galvanized spiral sheet-metal ventilation
  ductwork along an industrial ceiling, modern commercial building interior, soft daylight,
  photorealistic, no people". Used as the CTA-band background (under an accent gradient).
- `gen-kitchen-hood.png` — "interior of a large stainless steel commercial kitchen exhaust
  hood and ventilation canopy, freshly deep-cleaned, photorealistic, no people". Spare.
- `gen-blower-unit.png` — "close-up of a large industrial centrifugal extraction fan and
  blower unit, galvanized steel housing, clean mechanical room, photorealistic, no people".
  Spare.
- `gen-rooftop-hvac.png` — "industrial building rooftop with large galvanized steel HVAC
  ventilation ductwork and extraction fan units, golden hour, photorealistic, no people".
  Spare (was the hero before the twilight rooftop image).
- `gen-clean-duct.png` — "interior of a large clean stainless steel industrial kitchen
  exhaust duct after deep cleaning, photorealistic, no people". Spare / optional.

The hero originally used an Unsplash photo (Unsplash License); it is now an AI rooftop
image. The `images.unsplash.com` / `i.ytimg.com` remote patterns in `next.config.ts` are
currently unused.
